LM8000 Video Monitors

Takes advantage of a new processor able to output a video signal with the number of bits required by any FULL HD screen Takes advantage of a new processor able to output a video signal with the number of bits required by any FULL HD screen available in the market without bit truncation of the SDI input video signal.

Improved Color-depth of the displayed images, significantly improving the uniformity and linearity of the monitor as well as the quantity of colors that can be reproduced grows from 16.777.216 to 1.073.741.824.

Dual Input, Dual Output: Double video processor embedded into a single chip, able to show two identical images in parallel on the display (PbP) with the same type of de-interlacing, motion adaption, and scaling.

HDR Emulation: HDR display (HLG, PQ) for Rec709, DCI and Rec2020 color spaces, thanks to 3DLUT storing the tetrahedral interpolation of the images.
